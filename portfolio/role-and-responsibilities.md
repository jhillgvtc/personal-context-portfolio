# Role and Responsibilities

## Title
Manager, Ecommerce and Communications -- GVTC Communications

## How I Spend My Time

| Activity | % | Notes |
|----------|---|-------|
| Data analysis & modeling | 50-60% | SQL queries, Claude Code, propensity models, churn analysis, market research |
| Communications (email, Teams) | ~20% | Prefer batch-checking email in mornings only |

## What I Own End-to-End

- **Customer data analysis** -- the full picture: acquisition, churn, competitive dynamics, opportunities
- **Digital/ecommerce data** -- Google Analytics, online ordering funnel, conversion reporting
- **Market and external data** -- real estate trends, mortgage rates, competitive intel, housing activity
- **Paid digital advertising** -- Google Ads and Meta strategy, budget, vendor management
- **Ecommerce platform** -- responsible for converting sales through the NISC iVUE online ordering system
- **NPS and customer sentiment** -- qualitative analysis of survey data using Claude Code + SQL

## What I Deliver Regularly

- Monthly ecommerce performance reports (built in Claude Code)
- Propensity-to-buy analysis by product tier
- NPS sentiment analysis with theme extraction
- Ad-hoc strategic analysis for VP (customer growth, competitive response, penetration opportunities)
- Paid media campaign management and performance reporting
- Market/competitive intelligence briefings

## Direct Reports

- **Bree (Breanne)** -- Senior Communications Coordinator. Manages all traditional, internal, and external corporate communications.
- **Misty** -- Digital Strategist. Manages organic social, online reviews, customer sentiment, inbound social communications, and vendor coordination for paid social.

## Decision Authority

**I decide without approval:**
- Digital ad strategy, budget allocation, campaign targeting
- Which analyses to run and how to approach them
- Vendor selection and management for paid media
- Data methodology and reporting approach
- Tool adoption for my own workflow (Claude Code, AI tools)

**Needs sign-off:**
- Major vendor contracts (like Ping Marketing launch)
- Budget increases beyond allocated amounts
- Anything that touches brand positioning or public messaging strategy
- Organizational or headcount changes
