## Decision-Making Philosophy

Fast intuition, then validation. Gather inputs, decide quickly. Reversible decisions get made fast. Irreversible ones get more scrutiny.

When stuck: build a decision matrix, score things logically, or use AI to pressure-test assumptions. Don't sit on decisions.
